Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic Omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection,optionally, filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • total:Approximately 10min
  • Preparation:5minutes
  • Cooking:5minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespon of butter or oil
  • optional filling:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

Instructions

  • Beat the egg: in abowl the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are
    Well mixed.you can add a tables of water or milk for a fluffier texture

  • Heart the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

  • cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubling,pour in the eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

  • Add fillings(optional):Whwn the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly
    runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen filling over one half of the omelette.

  • Fold and serve:As the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it
    Over the fillings.let it cook for another minute,then slide it onto a plate.

  • Enjoy:Serve hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed.
